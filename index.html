Li Zhao 赵荔

I am a software engineer and author from the seaside tech hub of Shenzhen, China. I received a Bachelor of Arts degree from Pomona College in Claremont, California, where I majored in Computer Science and Mathematical Economics. Currently, I am an engineer at Laserfiche in Long Beach, California. My engineering interests include natural language processing and machine learning. I made an automated screenplay evaluation model for my undergrad thesis. I also made this Quora poem generator, inspired by this poem. Read my resume.

I have been a writer since age 7, publishing three fiction and one non-fiction book. I've also written TV animation scripts and songs. My work has been in a handful of anthologies and received some prizes. My favorite authors include John Steinbeck, Sylvia Plath, Katherine Mansfield, and Lu Xun. I enjoy reading traditional literature, detective fiction and ancient Chinese literature.

When I'm not writing code or novels, I make and eat food, and play guitar.
